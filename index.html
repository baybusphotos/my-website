Meet the Bay Bus

Once a humble bucket of rust, our 1973 VW bus has been lovingly restored and given a second life as a one-of-a-kind photo booth. With its timeless vintage charm and nostalgic flair, the bus brings a fun, unforgettable touch to weddings, birthdays, baby showers, corporate events, and more. Its retro style turns heads and sets the perfect backdrop for capturing memories. Beyond events, the bus also makes a unique prop for styled photoshoots, whether you're planning a romantic wedding proposal, a dreamy engagement session, or a creative gender reveal. Wherever it goes, this classic ride steals the spotlight and brings people together with smiles and snapshots.

How to Book

Booking the VW Photo Booth is a simple and collaborative process designed to make your experience fun and stress-free. To get started, just send us a message on Instagram, Facebook, or by email. From there, we’ll schedule a time to chat about your vision, confirm the date and location, and go over all the details. We’ll also create custom-designed photo strips tailored specifically to your event’s theme, colors, or branding. Once everything is confirmed, we handle the setup and breakdown, so all you have to do is enjoy the moment and let your guests have a blast capturing memories inside our one-of-a-kind vintage VW bus photo booth.
